Blogs

Blogs were a big part of my task at 3 Colours Rule. I wrote articles about different topics around branding, marketing and technology for educational and content marketing purposes.

Furthermore, writing blogs were effective for the SEO of 3CR, as the posts were optimised in readability and SEO. My articles can be found on the website of the agency.

The publication dates are from 1st March 2021 until 3rd January 2022. These posts were also turned into presentations to post on social media. Here are some specific topics I wrote about:

  • Find your USPs that resonate with your audience
  • How to align Sales with Marketing in your tech company
  • How to maximise your marketing efforts with a limited budget


Industry Report

Another piece of content that was published by me is the industry report, which was downloadable on the 3CR homepage. This report gave an overview of consumer drivers in the technology sector where one specific question was answered:

What drives consumers?

This is the most important question every business should ask itself when targeting an audience. Effectivity and efficiency is the key to successfully selling products and services at the right time to the right customer.

The digital era and the global pandemic COVID-19 have changed and transformed consumer drivers and behaviour.

The report – after reviewing content from analysts, surveys and industry experts – gives an overview of general information and growth strategies. It demonstrated the impact of COVID-19 that drives the consumers.
